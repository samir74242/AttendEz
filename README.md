# 📖 About ATTENDEZ

ATTENDEZ is a feature-rich Android application designed specifically for students who want an easier way to manage their academic life.

Instead of using multiple apps for attendance, notes, reminders, and timetables, ATTENDEZ brings everything together into one intuitive and modern platform.

Whether you're tracking attendance percentages, organizing class schedules, managing assignments, or taking multimedia notes, ATTENDEZ helps you stay focused on your studies without unnecessary complexity.
